\question A ball is thrown straight up into the air at a time $ t = 0 $. The height of the ball relative to the ground at any point in time is given by the function
\[
	p(t) = -t^2 + 6t + 7.
\]
\begin{enumerate}[label = \textbf{(\alph*)}]
    \item (6 points) For $t > 0$, find the instantaneous velocity of the object using the \underline{limit definition of the derivative}.
    \item (2 points) At what time does the ball first hit the ground?
    \item (2 points) What is the instantaneous velocity of the ball when it hits the ground in part (b)?
\end{enumerate}



\end{questions}
